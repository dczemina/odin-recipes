Cereal and Milk

Description

Return to your childhood with this timeless recipe. Bring back the days of waking up an eating a fresh bowl of cereal.

Ingredients

  • Cereal
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Choose the cereal that you want to eat. This is likely the most difficult step of the journey.
  2. Carefully open the box so as not to tear the tab that you will use to close the box later on. Nothing is more frustrating than stale cereal.
  3. Open the bag without spilling any of the precious cereal on the floor.
  4. Pour the cereal into your bowl.
  5. Douse the cereal with your chosen milk. Add a little extra if you want to have extra at the end.
  6. Enjoy!
