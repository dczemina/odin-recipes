Water

Description

Enjoyed for millenia by humans and animals alike, this timeless beverage is sure to please. Previously shrouded in mystery, the fog has finally been lifted. Follow the steps below and you'll be enjoying your own water in no time.

Materials

  • Cup or bowl

Steps

  1. Walk to your kitchen tap.
  2. Hold cup or bowl under the tap.
  3. Turn the tap on. Be sure to use the cold setting.
